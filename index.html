About me

A little bit about me

Think of me as a designer who loves solving problems, learning new things, and turning ideas into experiences people actually enjoy using.
From designing my first interface out of pure curiosity to working on real-world digital products used across industries, I've always been driven by one question — how can design make this better?
Over the years, I've worked with startups and global teams to build meaningful, user-centered products that balance beauty with functionality.
Whether it's crafting a design system, improving accessibility, or shaping a mobile app from concept to launch, I'm obsessed with details that make experiences feel natural.
When I'm not designing, you'll probably find me explaining design trends, mentoring other creatives, or refining my skills in front-end development and AI-assisted workflows.
Design, for me, isn't just work - it's how I explore the world
