KSE Website Revamp

Landing page website revamp for Kenya Society of Engineers

Miracle Onyinye
on Jan, 2025
Note: this product is in active development
Client
Kenya Society of Engineers (KSE) - The professional body regulating the engineering profession in Kenya.
Role
UI/UX Designer & Web Developer
Tools Used
Figma • Illustrator • Photoshop
Date
Jan 2025
Project Overview

The Kenya Society of Engineers (KSE) approached this revamp with the goal of modernizing their online presence. Their previous site was outdated and did not effectively represent the organization's credibility, values, or community of professionals.
The new design needed to:
  • Communicate authority and professionalism.
  • Improve user experience and content accessibility.
  • Strengthen brand identity across all pages
Objective

  • Redesign the entire KSE website with a modern, intuitive interface.
  • Enhance user navigation and overall content readability.
  • Showcase KSE's initiatives, news, and membership programs.
  • Ensure the platform is responsive and accessible on all devices
  • Establish visual consistency across the Home, About, and News pages
Design Process

Research & Discovery
  • I began with an audit of the existing site to identify usability issues and outdated design elements. I benchmarked leading professional associations for design inspiration and best practices - focusing on structure trust-building and clarity.
Wireframing & Structure
  • Developed low-fidelity wireframes to restructure content and establish intuitive navigation paths. The goal was to reduce clutter and present information in a professional yet engaging layout.
UI Design
  • Focused on clean lines, balanced typography, and color contrast. The design used KSE's signature blue and gold for brand consistency, complemented by white space for a polished and breathable feel.
Implementation
  • Once designs were finalized, the site was developed to ensure pixel-perfect execution and optional performance across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

Visual Identity & UI Highlights

Typography: Modern sans-serif for readability and professionalism.
Color Palette: Blue and Gold tones symbolizing trust and excellence.
Layout: Consistent grid system, generous white space, and clean composition.
Imagery: Contextual and human-centered to reinforce credibility.
Results

Improved Engagemment: Enhanced visual appeal and accessibility led to better user interaction.
Stronger Brand Trust: The updated design aligns with KSE's mission and public reputation.
Modern & Responsive: Fully adaptable design with KSE's mission and public reputation.
Clearer Communication: Structured content flow improved information delivery and user understanding.
