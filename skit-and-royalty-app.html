Skit Store, Rewards & Loyalty App

A rewards and loyalty application designed to increase customer retention, repeat purchases, and long-term brand engagement

Miracle Onyinye
2023
In 2023, I designed a rewards and loyalty application for Skit Store aimed at increasing customer retention, repeat purchases, and long-term brand engagement. The goal was to move beyond traditional discount-based loyalty programs and create a system that genuinely rewards customer behavior while remaining simple, transparent, and motivating.
Rather than treating rewards as an afterthought, the app was designed as a core engagement layer within Skit Store’s ecosystem, one that turns everyday actions like shopping, referrals, and engagement into tangible value for customers. I led the end-to-end product design, including research, user flows, reward logic, interaction design, and high-fidelity UI.
The Challenge

Customer loyalty is difficult to sustain, especially in competitive retail environments.

Key challenges identified included:

1. Low Repeat Purchases
Customers often shop once and disengage, especially when incentives feel generic or short-lived.

2. Reward Fatigue
Traditional point systems can feel unclear, slow, or unrewarding if value isn’t communicated properly.

3. Transparency & Trust
Users need to understand exactly how rewards are earned, redeemed, and expired.

4. Balancing Business Goals
The system had to encourage spending and engagement without eroding profit margins.

5. Engagement Beyond Discounts
Skit Store wanted loyalty driven by experience, not just price cuts.
Research & Insights

To design an effective rewards experience, I studied:

Retail Loyalty Apps
Analyzing how leading brands structure points, tiers, and redemption flows.

Gamification Systems
Understanding how progress indicators, milestones, and achievements drive motivation.

User Behavior Patterns
Insights revealed that:
  • Users engage more when rewards feel attainable
  • Visual progress increases motivation
  • Clear redemption paths build trust
These findings guided a design focused on clarity, motivation, and long-term engagement.

Learnings

Rewards Must Feel Achievable
Users disengage when rewards feel too far away.

Clarity Builds Trust
Transparent rules and visible progress improve long-term adoption.

Gamification Drives Retention
Small wins and milestones keep users coming back.

Loyalty Is Emotional, Not Just Transactional
Recognition and progression matter as much as discounts.
